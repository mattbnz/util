How to play

  1. On your phone (or any Spotify device), open a playlist and turn on shuffle, then hit play.
  2. Come back here. Click Start next round: the server skips to the next song and records it as the round's answer.
  3. Players answer at the root URL. The round ends once half have answered, or you click End round now.
